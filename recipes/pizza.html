Pizza

Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim – and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame. Learn how to make the best pizza crust of your life with just a few ingredients, find out how to shape the dough, and get our best storage secrets.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare pizza dough by combining flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil.
  2. Knead the dough until smooth, then let it rise for about an hour until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible, typically around 475°F to 500°F (245°C to 260°C).
  4. Roll out the dough into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  5. Place the dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  7. Add desired toppings such as cheese, vegetables, meats, and herbs.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy!
